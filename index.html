How to Play the Number Guessing Game

  1. Start a New Game:
    • Click on the "Again!" button to reset the game and start fresh. The game will randomly generate a new secret number between 1 and 20.
    • The number of attempts you have is displayed as "score," and it will start at 5.
  2. Make a Guess:
    • Enter your guess (a number between 1 and 20) in the input box and click on the "Check!" button.
    • If you do not enter a number, you will see the message "NO NUMBER".
  3. Receive Feedback:
    • If your guess is correct, the message will change to "CORRECT NUMBER!!!!!ðŸ¥³". The background color will turn green, and your score will be doubled.
    • If your guess is too high, the message will display "HIGH". Your number of attempts will decrease by one.
    • If your guess is too low, the message will display "LOW". Your number of attempts will decrease by one.
  4. End of Game:
    • If you guess the correct number within the allowed attempts, your score will be displayed and compared to the high score.
    • If your score exceeds the high score, it will be updated.
    • If you run out of attempts, the message "YOU LOSTðŸ˜”" will be displayed, and the background will turn red.
